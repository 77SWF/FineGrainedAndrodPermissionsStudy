<p>The main purpose of providing a ClipData is to allow granting of
 URI permissions for data associated with the clip.  The exact kind
 of permission grant to perform is specified through <var translate="no">grantFlags</var>.

 </p><table class="responsive">
    <tbody><tr><th colspan="2">Parameters</th></tr>
      <tr>
        <td><code dir="ltr" translate="no">clip</code></td>
        <td width="100%">
          <code dir="ltr" translate="no">ClipData</code>: The new clip to set.  May be null to clear the current clip.<p></p></td>
      </tr>
      <tr>
        <td><code dir="ltr" translate="no">grantFlags</code></td>
        <td width="100%">
          <code dir="ltr" translate="no">int</code>: The desired permissions to grant for any URIs.  This should be
 a combination of <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/content/Intent#FLAG_GRANT_READ_URI_PERMISSION">Intent.FLAG_GRANT_READ_URI_PERMISSION</a></code>,
 <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/content/Intent#FLAG_GRANT_WRITE_URI_PERMISSION">Intent.FLAG_GRANT_WRITE_URI_PERMISSION</a></code>, and
 <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/content/Intent#FLAG_GRANT_PREFIX_URI_PERMISSION">Intent.FLAG_GRANT_PREFIX_URI_PERMISSION</a></code>.<p></p></td>
      </tr>
    </tbody></table>
