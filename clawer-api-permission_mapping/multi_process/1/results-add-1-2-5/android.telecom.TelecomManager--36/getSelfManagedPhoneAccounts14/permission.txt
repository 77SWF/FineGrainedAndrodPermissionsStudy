<p>
 Requires permission <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/Manifest.permission#READ_PHONE_STATE">Manifest.permission.READ_PHONE_STATE</a></code>, or that the caller
 is the default dialer app to get all phone account handles.
 </p><p>
 If the caller doesn't meet any of the above requirements and has <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/Manifest.permission#MANAGE_OWN_CALLS">Manifest.permission.MANAGE_OWN_CALLS</a></code>, the caller can get only the phone account
 handles they have registered.
 </p><p>
 A <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/java/lang/SecurityException">SecurityException</a></code> will be thrown if the caller is not the default dialer
 or the caller does not have at least one of the following permissions:
 <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/Manifest.permission#READ_PHONE_STATE">Manifest.permission.READ_PHONE_STATE</a></code> permission,
 <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/Manifest.permission#MANAGE_OWN_CALLS">Manifest.permission.MANAGE_OWN_CALLS</a></code> permission

 <br/>
 Requires android.Manifest.permission.READ_PRIVILEGED_PHONE_STATE or <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/Manifest.permission#READ_PHONE_STATE">Manifest.permission.READ_PHONE_STATE</a></code> or <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/Manifest.permission#MANAGE_OWN_CALLS">Manifest.permission.MANAGE_OWN_CALLS</a></code></p>
