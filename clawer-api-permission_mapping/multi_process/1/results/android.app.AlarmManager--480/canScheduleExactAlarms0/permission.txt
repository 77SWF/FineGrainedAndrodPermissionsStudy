<p>
 Apps targeting <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/os/Build.VERSION_CODES#S">Build.VERSION_CODES#S</a></code> or higher can schedule exact alarms only if they
 have the <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/Manifest.permission#SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM">Manifest.permission#SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM</a></code> permission or they are on the
 device's power-save exemption list.
 These apps can also
 start <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/provider/Settings#ACTION_REQUEST_SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM">Settings.ACTION_REQUEST_SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM</a></code> to
 request this permission from the user.
 </p>
