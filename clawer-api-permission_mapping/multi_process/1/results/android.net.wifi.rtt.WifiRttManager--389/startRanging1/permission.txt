<p>
 Ranging request with only Wifi Aware peers can be performed with either
 <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/Manifest.permission#NEARBY_WIFI_DEVICES">Manifest.permission.NEARBY_WIFI_DEVICES</a></code> with
 android:usesPermissionFlags="neverForLocation", or
 <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/Manifest.permission#ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION">Manifest.permission.ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION</a></code>. All other types of ranging requests
 require <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/Manifest.permission#ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION">Manifest.permission.ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION</a></code>.

 Requires <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/Manifest.permission#ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION">Manifest.permission.ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION</a></code> and <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/Manifest.permission#CHANGE_WIFI_STATE">Manifest.permission.CHANGE_WIFI_STATE</a></code> and <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/Manifest.permission#ACCESS_WIFI_STATE">Manifest.permission.ACCESS_WIFI_STATE</a></code> and <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/Manifest.permission#NEARBY_WIFI_DEVICES">Manifest.permission.NEARBY_WIFI_DEVICES</a></code></p>
