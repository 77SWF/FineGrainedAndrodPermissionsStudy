<p>Registers a GNSS status callback. GNSS status information will only be received while the
 <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/location/LocationManager#GPS_PROVIDER">GPS_PROVIDER</a></code> is enabled, and while the client app is in the foreground.
