<p>
 Applications should have the
 <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/Manifest.permission#ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION">Manifest.permission.ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION</a></code> and
 <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/Manifest.permission#ACCESS_WIFI_STATE">Manifest.permission.ACCESS_WIFI_STATE</a></code> permissions.
 Callers without the permission will trigger a <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/java/lang/SecurityException">SecurityException</a></code>.
 </p><p>
