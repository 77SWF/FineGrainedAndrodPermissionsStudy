<p>Return list of all URI permission grants that have been persisted by the
 calling app. That is, the returned permissions have been granted
 <em>to</em> the calling app. Only persistable grants taken with
 <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/content/ContentResolver#takePersistableUriPermission(android.net.Uri,%20int)">takePersistableUriPermission(android.net.Uri, int)</a></code> are returned.
 </p>
