<p>
 Any app can <em>accept</em> blame for traffic performed on a socket
 originally created by another app by calling this method with the
 <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/system/Os#getuid()">Os.getuid()</a></code> value. However, only apps holding the
 <code dir="ltr" translate="no">android.Manifest.permission#UPDATE_DEVICE_STATS</code> permission may
 <em>assign</em> blame to another UIDs.
