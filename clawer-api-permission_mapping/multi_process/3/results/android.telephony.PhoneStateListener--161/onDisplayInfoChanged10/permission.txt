<p> The <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/telephony/TelephonyDisplayInfo">TelephonyDisplayInfo</a></code> contains status information shown to the user based on
 carrier policy.

 For clients compiled on Android 11 SDK, requires permission:
 <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/Manifest.permission#READ_PHONE_STATE">Manifest.permission.READ_PHONE_STATE</a></code> or that the calling app has carrier
 privileges (see <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/telephony/TelephonyManager#hasCarrierPrivileges()">TelephonyManager#hasCarrierPrivileges</a></code>).
 For clients compiled on Android 12 SDK or newer,
 <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/Manifest.permission#READ_PHONE_STATE">Manifest.permission.READ_PHONE_STATE</a></code> or carrier privileges is not required
 anymore.
