<p> A caller requires permissions to access the Credentials
 in the returned <code dir="ltr" translate="no">Set</code>, or to modify the
 <code dir="ltr" translate="no">Set</code> itself.  A <code dir="ltr" translate="no">SecurityException</code>
 is thrown if the caller does not have the proper permissions.

 </p><p> While iterating through the <code dir="ltr" translate="no">Set</code>,
 a <code dir="ltr" translate="no">SecurityException</code> is thrown
 if the caller does not have permission to access a
 particular Credential.  The <code dir="ltr" translate="no">Iterator</code>
 is nevertheless advanced to next element in the <code dir="ltr" translate="no">Set</code>.

 </p>
