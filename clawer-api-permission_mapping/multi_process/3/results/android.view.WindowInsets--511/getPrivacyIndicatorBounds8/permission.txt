<p>Returns the <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/graphics/Rect">Rect</a></code> of the maximum bounds of the system privacy indicator, for the
 current orientation, in relative coordinates, or null if the bounds have not been loaded yet.
 The privacy indicator shows over apps when an app uses the microphone or camera permissions,
 while an app is in immersive mode.</p>
