<p>On Android <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/os/Build.VERSION_CODES#Q">Build.VERSION_CODES#Q</a></code> and later devices, <code dir="ltr" translate="no">token</code> cannot be
 <code dir="ltr" translate="no">null</code> even with <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/Manifest.permission#WRITE_SECURE_SETTINGS">Manifest.permission.WRITE_SECURE_SETTINGS</a></code>. Instead,
 update <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/provider/Settings.Secure#DEFAULT_INPUT_METHOD">Settings.Secure.DEFAULT_INPUT_METHOD</a></code> and
 <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/provider/Settings.Secure#SELECTED_INPUT_METHOD_SUBTYPE">Settings.Secure.SELECTED_INPUT_METHOD_SUBTYPE</a></code> directly.</p>
