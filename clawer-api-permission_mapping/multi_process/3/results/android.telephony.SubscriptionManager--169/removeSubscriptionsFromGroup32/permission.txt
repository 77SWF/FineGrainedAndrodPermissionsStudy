 See <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/telephony/SubscriptionManager#createSubscriptionGroup(java.util.List&lt;java.lang.Integer&gt;)">createSubscriptionGroup(java.util.List)</a></code> for more details.

 <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/telephony/TelephonyManager#hasCarrierPrivileges()">TelephonyManager#hasCarrierPrivileges()</a></code> or
