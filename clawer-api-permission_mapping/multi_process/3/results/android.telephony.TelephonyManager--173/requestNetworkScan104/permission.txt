<p>Requires Permission:
 <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/Manifest.permission#MODIFY_PHONE_STATE">MODIFY_PHONE_STATE</a></code> or that the calling
 app has carrier privileges (see <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/telephony/TelephonyManager#hasCarrierPrivileges()">hasCarrierPrivileges()</a></code>)
 and <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/Manifest.permission#ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION">Manifest.permission.ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION</a></code>.

 If the system-wide location switch is off, apps may still call this API, with the
 following constraints:
 </p><ol>
     <li>The app must hold the <code dir="ltr" translate="no">android.permission.NETWORK_SCAN</code> permission.</li>
     <li>The app must not supply any specific bands or channels to scan.</li>
     <li>The app must only specify MCC/MNC pairs that are
     associated to a SIM in the device.</li>
     <li>Returned results will have no meaningful info other than signal strength
     and MCC/MNC info.</li>
