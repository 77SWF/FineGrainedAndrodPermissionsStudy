 If the caller has carrier priviliges on any active subscription, then they have permission to
 get simple information like the card ID (<code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/telephony/UiccCardInfo#getCardId()">UiccCardInfo#getCardId()</a></code>), whether the card
 is an eUICC (<code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/telephony/UiccCardInfo#isEuicc()">UiccCardInfo#isEuicc()</a></code>), and the physical slot index where the card is
 inserted (<code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/telephony/UiccCardInfo#getPhysicalSlotIndex()">UiccCardInfo#getPhysicalSlotIndex()</a></code>.
 </p><p>
