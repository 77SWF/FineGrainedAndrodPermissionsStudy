<p>Requires one of the following permissions:
 <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/Manifest.permission#ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE">Manifest.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE</a></code>,
 <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/Manifest.permission#MODIFY_PHONE_STATE">Manifest.permission.MODIFY_PHONE_STATE</a></code>, or
 <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/Manifest.permission#READ_BASIC_PHONE_STATE">READ_BASIC_PHONE_STATE</a></code> or that the calling app has carrier
 privileges (see <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/telephony/TelephonyManager#hasCarrierPrivileges()">hasCarrierPrivileges()</a></code>).

 </p><p>Note that this does not take into account any data restrictions that may be present on the
