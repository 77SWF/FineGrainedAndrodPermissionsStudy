<p>Per the Android CDD, only biometric authenticators that meet or exceed the requirements
 for <strong>Strong</strong> are permitted to integrate with Keystore to perform related
 cryptographic operations. Therefore, it is an error to call this method after explicitly
 calling <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/hardware/biometrics/BiometricPrompt.Builder#setAllowedAuthenticators(int)">Builder#setAllowedAuthenticators(int)</a></code> with any biometric strength other than
 <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/hardware/biometrics/BiometricManager.Authenticators#BIOMETRIC_STRONG">Authenticators#BIOMETRIC_STRONG</a></code>.

 <br/>
 Requires <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/Manifest.permission#USE_BIOMETRIC">Manifest.permission.USE_BIOMETRIC</a></code></p>
