<p> <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/bluetooth/BluetoothProfile#EXTRA_STATE">BluetoothProfile.EXTRA_STATE</a></code> will transition from
 <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/bluetooth/BluetoothHeadset#STATE_AUDIO_CONNECTING">STATE_AUDIO_CONNECTING</a></code> to <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/bluetooth/BluetoothHeadset#STATE_AUDIO_CONNECTED">STATE_AUDIO_CONNECTED</a></code> when
 audio connection is established and to <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/bluetooth/BluetoothHeadset#STATE_AUDIO_DISCONNECTED">STATE_AUDIO_DISCONNECTED</a></code>
 in case of failure to establish the audio connection.

 <br/>
 For apps targeting <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/os/Build.VERSION_CODES#R">Build.VERSION_CODES#R</a></code> or lower, this
 requires the <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/Manifest.permission#BLUETOOTH">Manifest.permission#BLUETOOTH</a></code> permission
 which can be gained with a simple <code dir="ltr" translate="no">&lt;uses-permission&gt;</code>
 manifest tag.
 <br/>
 For apps targeting <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/os/Build.VERSION_CODES#S">Build.VERSION_CODES#S</a></code> or or higher,
 this requires the <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/Manifest.permission#BLUETOOTH_CONNECT">Manifest.permission#BLUETOOTH_CONNECT</a></code>
 permission which can be gained with
 <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/app/Activity#requestPermissions(java.lang.String[],%20int)">Activity.requestPermissions(String[], int)</a></code>.
 <br/>
 Requires <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/Manifest.permission#BLUETOOTH_CONNECT">Manifest.permission.BLUETOOTH_CONNECT</a></code> and <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/Manifest.permission#MODIFY_PHONE_STATE">Manifest.permission.MODIFY_PHONE_STATE</a></code></p>
