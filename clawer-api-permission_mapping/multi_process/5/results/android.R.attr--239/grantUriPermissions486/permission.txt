<p>If true, the <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/content/Context#grantUriPermission(java.lang.String,%20android.net.Uri,%20int)">Context.grantUriPermission</a></code> or corresponding Intent flags can
 be used to allow others to access specific URIs in the content
 provider, even if they do not have an explicit read or write
 permission.  If you are supporting this feature, you must be
 sure to call <code dir="ltr" translate="no"><a href="/reference/android/content/Context#revokeUriPermission(android.net.Uri,%20int)">Context.revokeUriPermission</a></code> when URIs are deleted from your
 provider.
 </p>
